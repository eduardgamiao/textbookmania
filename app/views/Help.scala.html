General Use

Use the interactive Nav-Bar to visit other pages of the site.

Student Profiles

After hitting the 'Students' tab in the Nav-Bar, it will take you to a page that displays a table of Students currently in the database.


Adding a Profile

Click the 'Add Student' button to add your own Student Profile.


You will be re-directed to a new page where you can fill out your details.


Textbooks

After hitting the 'Textbooks' tab in the Nav-Bar, it will take you to a page that displays a table of Textbooks currently in our database.


Adding a Textbook

Click the 'Add Textbook' button to add Textbook that is not in our database.


You will be re-directed to a new page where you can fill out the textbook details.


Marketplace

The 'Marketplace' tab will offer three choices: Buying, Selling, and Matches.


Buying and Selling

In the 'Buying' and 'Selling' pages, it will display a table of Buy-Offers and Sell-offers on textbooks made by other students.

Add an Offer

To add you own offer, click the 'Add Offer' button.

You will be re-directed to a new page where you can fill out your offer details.


Matches

The 'Matches' page will display the Buy-Offers and Sell-Offers of a selected Student.

To find matches, select a Student in the and click 'Find Matches'.
